Mobile First Web Design

We offer Mobile First responsive website design and web development services as standard: a modern approach to web design and website development. Responsive website design is a modern approach to web design which makes websites look great on a variety of devices or screen sizes. The website adapts to the screen size of the device, providing a great user experience on any device or web browser. Meaning that your customers will not have to squeeze or resize your website on mobile or tablet devices to view the information on your site clearly. Immediately improving the experience of your website and brand association. This approach also assists in search engine performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

We pride ourselves in being transparent. Below are Frequently Asked Questions. However, we are always happy to answer your enquiries before or during a website build. You can call us up, send an email or complete the contact Us form.

  • What if I already have a domain name?

    No problem. We can help you to point your domain name to your new website or assist you to move your domain to our registrar. Some people prefer to keep everything in one place, it's your domain name to do as you wish.

  • Will I own my website?

    Yes. Once the payment has completed, you will own your website. You can choose to continue with us or move your website elsewhere. Customers stay with us from the value we add due to the 20+ years experience in actually running website businesses. We add value to their businesses through planning, consultancy and marketing services.

  • Is there a contract?

    No, However, once we start we would agree an upfront payment as a percentage of the agreed website cost. We put in a huge amount of upfront work for your business and break payments into affordable chunks agreed at the start. Marketing, design & consultancy services also do not have a contract and are paid ad-hoc or month to month and can be cancelled at any time.

  • How are payments taken?

    We will generate an invoice for any agreed services. All payments are processed through a secure card processor such as Stripe or Paypal, unless agreed otherwise.We never have your card details to ensure that they are secure.
